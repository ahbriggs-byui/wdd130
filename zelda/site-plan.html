Content

Home page

Welcome to Hyrule Compendium! A site for all Zelda nerds and curious adventures alike. We specialize in game facts and timeline explanation. Having trouble explaining the convoluted timeline to your poor uncultured friends? Well look no further because we have it all right here! Ever curious on when certain games were released? Or even what console they were released on? The Game Information page has all the answers to the never ending questions you desire.
